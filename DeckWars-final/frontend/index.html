DECK WARS

Collect NFT warrior cards. Challenge opponents to 1v1 battles. Win with skill and strategy in a provably fair, on-chain trading card game.

🃏 Open Collection ⚔️ Enter Battle 💧 Get Testnet HLUSD
🃏

NFT Cards

Each warrior is a unique ERC-721 NFT on HeLa chain. 5 classes, 10 card types with distinct stats — warriors, mages, rogues, paladins, and rangers.

🔐

Commit-Reveal Fairness

Rolling commit-reveal scheme ensures provably fair randomness. No player can cheat — both must commit their move hash before revealing, creating tamper-proof outcomes.

⚡

Speed-Based Turns

Faster cards act first. Crits, misses, and damage variance are derived from combined player seeds — fair, random, and verifiable on-chain.

🧩

Composable Moves

Each move is its own smart contract. The game is fully extensible — Basic Attack, Heavy Strike, Shield, and Heal are just the start. Anyone can deploy new moves.

💰

Wager & Win

Stake HLUSD when you issue a challenge. Winner takes the full pot. A timeout system prevents griefing — act within 5 minutes or forfeit.

🔗

HeLa Blockchain

Built on HeLa's high-performance EVM-compatible Layer-1. Fast finality, low gas, and a full block explorer to verify every battle on-chain.

How to Play

From wallet connect to on-chain victory

STEP 1

Connect MetaMask and add HeLa Testnet (RPC: testnet-rpc.helachain.com, Chain ID: 666888)

STEP 2

Mint 3 NFT cards from the Collection page. Each card has unique stats and 4 moves. Mix classes for best strategy.

STEP 3

Issue a challenge with an opponent's address and set your HLUSD wager. Approve your 3 cards for battle.

STEP 4

Each turn: commit your move secretly, wait for opponent, then both reveal. Watch the battle resolve on-chain!
